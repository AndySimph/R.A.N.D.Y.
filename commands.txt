Commands:

:}help - Displays commands
:}p (youtube url) - Plays youtube video in current voice channel
:}pause - Pauses current song
:}Resume - Resumes the paused song
:}vol (0-200) - Sets current volume
:}skip - Skips current youtube video
:}se (...) - Searches and displays first 10 videos on youtube to play
:}kill - Kills R.A.N.D.Y.
:}jojo - Outputs a random Jojo quote
:}purge - Deletes messages in bulk
:}hook (...) - Uses the webhook to send messages
